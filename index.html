About EmpowerHER

EmpowerHER is a three-day leadership summit presented by the Women In Signal Processing (WiSP) wing of IEEE SPS Kerala Chapter and the WiSP representatives of all the SBCs across the Kerala section, exclusively designed for IEEE WiE volunteers across IEEE Kerala Section. This transformative event encompasses a series of dynamic leadership training programs in various domains, featuring inspiring panel discussions with accomplished women entrepreneurs, engineers, professors, scientists, industrialists, and influencers.

Federal Institute of Science and Technology,Angamaly

Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

FISAT,Angamaly,Ernakulam

FISAT is a renowned institution known for its commitment to excellence in education and fostering a vibrant academic environment. With state-of-the-art facilities, a dedicated faculty, and a student body brimming with talent, FISAT has been selected as the ideal host for EmpowerHER. Their strong track record in organizing successful events, coupled with their enthusiasm and passion for creating memorable experiences, ensures that EmpowerHER will be a grand success under their able leadership. FISAT's location in Angamaly, a bustling town in Ernakulam district, offers convenient access and a vibrant atmosphere for participants to immerse themselves in the event's activities. With FISAT as the host, participants can expect an exceptional event filled with innovation, learning, and networking opportunities.
